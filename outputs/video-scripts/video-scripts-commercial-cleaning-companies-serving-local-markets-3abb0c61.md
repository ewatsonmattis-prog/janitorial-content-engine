# Video Scripts: Commercial cleaning companies serving local markets



## Script 1: LinkedIn Video (~82s)

**Hook:** Facilities managers are ignoring your cold emails — here's exactly why.

[0–3 seconds] Facilities managers are ignoring your cold emails — here's exactly why.

[3–15 seconds] You're sending cold emails to facilities managers and getting nothing back. No replies, no calls, no contracts. And it's not because they don't need a cleaning company — it's because your email looks exactly like the last ten they deleted.

[15–55 seconds] Facilities managers shortlist contractors based on two things: responsiveness and specificity. If your email doesn't reference their industry, their building type, or their likely cleaning schedule — it reads as spam. What actually works is a 3-email sequence. Email one references their sector — office, healthcare, or education — and a specific pain point for that environment. Email two follows up with a concrete offer tied to their building size. Email three is a short, direct close. Each email does one job. No long pitches. No brochure attachments.

[55–70 seconds] This kind of targeted sequence converts at three to five times the rate of a generic pitch — because it signals that you actually understand their operation before you've even walked through the door.
